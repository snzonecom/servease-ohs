Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service? +

1. Sign up or log in to your Servease account.
2. Choose the service you need.
3. Select a provider and schedule a booking.
4. Submit your booking request and wait for provider confirmation.

How are service providers verified? +

The service providers listed on our platform went to screening and verification through a background check, identity verification to ensure quality and safety of everyone. Customer reviews were also displayed for you to refer.

Can I cancel or reschedule a booking? +

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule a booking when your booking is still in pending status. Visit your booking history to manage your appointments. Cancellation policies may vary depending on the service provider.

What payment methods does Servease accept? +

Cash is the only mode of payment as of the moment. However, more payment options will be added soon.

Is Servease available in my area? +

Currently, Servease operates in areas of Pampanga. We are working on expanding to more areas soon!
